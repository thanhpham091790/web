Top three activities to do in Hanoi

Street food

Hanoi’s street food ranges from a collection of slightly grotty roadside tables and low stools to nicer looking eateries with bathrooms. Most of the time, you grab any available seat and then order what you want to eat

Water pupet

The art form of water puppetry originated at least 1,000 years ago in the rice fields of north Vietnam. Particularly if you’re traveling with kids, you’d be remiss to leave Hanoi without catching a show at the Thang Long Water Puppet Theatre

Opera house

French colonists in Vietnam wanted to enjoy the same leisure time activities in Asia that they had loved in France. This is a smaller version of the famous Opera House in Paris, built between 1857-74 and designed by Charles Garnier.
